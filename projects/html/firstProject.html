A few facts about the world we live in!

Earth is the third planet form the Sun and is the largest of the terrestrial planets. Unlike the other planets in the solar system that are named after classic deities, the Earth's name comes from the Anglo-Saxon word erda which means ground or soil. The Earth was formed approximately 4.54 billion years ago and is the only known planet to support life.
